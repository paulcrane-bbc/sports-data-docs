Points scored in each period. This is not cumulative.

E.g., imagine a game in play in the second quarter: Team A scores 12 points in the first quarter and 6 points so far in the second quarter. firstQuarter will equal '12'. secondQuarter will equal '6' and score (level above) will equal '18'
